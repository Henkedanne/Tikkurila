ABOUT

We are team of students from the digital media school Hyper Island in Stockholm. In the start of 2017, we got a brief from Tikkurila – one of the leading paint companies in Northern Europe. The purpose was to create a colorful experience for the brand. In other words, they asked us what they would do if they didn’t do paint. Here is our solution.
